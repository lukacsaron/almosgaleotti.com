## Image Specifications

### General Guidelines

**File Formats:**
- Photographs: JPEG (quality 85-90%)
- Graphics with transparency: PNG
- Optimize all images for web (use ImageOptim, TinyPNG, or similar)

**Color Profile:**
- sRGB (standard web color space)
- Avoid overly saturated colors
- Warm, natural tones preferred

**Style Consistency:**
- Professional but approachable
- Clean, uncluttered compositions
- Natural lighting preferred over harsh flash
- Warm color palette (terracotta, sage, cream tones)

**Mobile Optimization:**
- All images should look good when cropped for mobile (portrait cards become smaller)
- Key subject matter should be centered or safe from edge cropping

### Aspect Ratios by Card Type

- **Portrait cards** (row-span-2): 2:3 or 3:4 ratio
- **Landscape cards** (span-2): 16:9 or 3:2 ratio
- **Square cards**: 1:1 ratio
- **Row-span-12 cards** (tall portraits): 2:3 ratio
